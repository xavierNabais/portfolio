ABOUT ME

My name is Xavier and I have been a digital enthusiast since childhood. From an early age, I explored internet browsing and played many games, which allowed me to develop various skills and understand the programming behind them. In addition, I acquired extensive knowledge on how to use websites, as well as a great deal of experience in user interface, easily distinguishing between good and bad user experiences.

In high school, I enrolled in a multimedia course, where I deepened my knowledge and interest in programming and web development. I acquired the basics of programming, from algorithms to programming structures, and began working as a freelance web developer, where I successfully completed some projects.

Later, I took a technical specialization course in multimedia, which provided me with solid foundations in important areas such as design and marketing. Currently, I am in the last semester of my bachelor’s degree in multimedia, where I continue to develop my skills and deepen my knowledge in web development.

I am certain that I want to pursue a career in the field of web development and I am excited to continue evolving in this constantly changing area. My passion for technology and its possibilities is what motivates me to be an excellent professional in this ever-evolving field.
